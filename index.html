About Me

Hello, my name is Jessie Won and I'm currently studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I'm an aspiring engineer and designer, but first and foremost, am a student who loves to learn!

I spend most of my days working towards my degree, and am especially interested in learning and understanding the design process and its applications. I love a good challenge, and am eager to continue exploring the different aspects of engineering.

Aside from school, I like to keep myself pretty busy. Athletics have always been a sigificant part of my life, whether it was competing in biathlon and sailing, or going snowboarding with my friends. I love the routine and challenges sports demand, and the incomparable team atmosphere it inspires. I'm currently training 15-20 hours a week as a member of the University of Waterloo Varsity Swim Team, and also work as a part-time sailing instructor for Cadets Canada. However, my favourite past-time is watching Food Network and trying new restaurants, but when time permits, I do love a good adventure. From week-long canoe expeditions and 11-hour hikes to an impulsive half marathon, I live for exciting experiences and am always up to try something new.

TL;DR

When I'm not working, I'm definitely out and about looking for a good time; you'll probably find me thinking about food while outside playing sports or planning my next trip.

PATH

App Design

At Finhacks, my team and I developed an Android application, Path. Path is a navigation tool that outputted the best public transit route based on the different tasks and the estimated time needed at each location, as inputed by the user. Using the Google Maps API, we cross referenced public transit schedules, and outputted the most efficient route and order of task completion. I worked predominantly on the UI, designing the different mockups.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
Languages Used: Java
